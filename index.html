Key Features

Low Risk

MMFs invest in secure, short-term assets, reducing exposure to market volatility.

High Liquidity

Investors can typically access their money easily, within a few days.

Professional Management

Funds are managed by financial experts who seek to optimize returns while minimizing risks.

Targeted Value

MMFs generally aim to maintain a stable $1 Net Asset Value (NAV) per share, with income distributed as dividends.

Accessibility

Retail MMFs have low minimum investment requirements, making them accessible to individuals.
